# Design & Style Guidelines

Based on `design-charter.md`:

## Vision & Vibe
- **Theme:** High-End Space Engineering / "Mission Control"
- **Atmosphere:** Dark, moody, cinematic, technical, precise.
- **Narrative:** "Scroll-scripted" starship journey.

## Core Constraints ("Never" List)
- **NO Glassmorphism:** Avoid translucent backgrounds. Use solid OLED blacks and hex/neon borders.
- **NO Astrology:** Use technical telemetry/structure instead.
- **NO Percentage Bars:** Use "Data Analysis" visuals.
- **NO Manual Carousels:** Use "Sticky-Scroll" storytelling.

## Design System
- **Background:** OLED Black (`#050505`)
- **Accents:** 
  - Neon Purple (`#A855F7`) - Primary CTA/Border
  - Satellite Blue (`#0EA5E9`) - Nav/Data Lines
- **Surface:** Solid Dark Gray (`#111111`)

## Typography
- **Headlines:** Inter or Montserrat (Bold, All-Caps)
- **Data/Code:** JetBrains Mono or IBM Plex Mono (Regular)

## UI Patterns
- **Starship Anchor:** Minimalist ship fixed to center.
- **Sticky Scroll Sections:** 100vh sections with fade transitions.
- **Mission Data Panels:** Sharp corners (0px/2px), solid black with neon stroke.
